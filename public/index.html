About our coffee

It's no secret that there are many different types of coffee, and each has its own ideal taste. If you haven't found yours yet, don't be upset. Below we offer a brief description of two well-known varieties.

Robusta

Arabica

Arabica is the most famous and popular coffee in the world. Depending on where it is harvested and how the coffee is processed, Arabica coffee can open up with completely different flavors. The caffeine content in Arabica is much less than in Robusta, so Arabica has a milder taste and subtle aroma.
